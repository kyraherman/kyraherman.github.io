In the past decades, Nashville has become more popular for food choice. Since 2015 over ten thousand restaurants and bars have produced a big amount of sales. The National Restaurant Association concluded around $11.7 billion alone was made in Nashville.

Tourists and guests of the city have lots of different options if they are feeling hungry. If you are looking for something quick and easy, more fast food, the popular Five Guys is a huge hit. Other chain places include Cook Out, Pita Pit, and Fat Mo’s. For more formal dining or a fancy night out, Etch, Catbird Seat, or Capitol Grille are a few. Keep in mind some of the dining places do have higher prices if they are in the center of downtown.
