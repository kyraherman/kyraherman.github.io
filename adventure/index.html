A Look At the City

IMAGE

August 16 2019

Today we drove into Madison Wisconsin right down East Washington Avenue. I could see the top of the capitaol a few miles before reaching the downtown. It was beautiful! The first adventure of the day was visiting the Monona Terrace. Thankfully I brought a nice quality camera along because the terrace chairs were so vibrant, colorful, very unique. On top of all the exciting new attractions, the weather made it a perfect day. Unfortunately, I forgot sunglasses, so if you plan on strolling Madtown (the nickname college students give their city) for the day, remember a pair of shades.

State street is exactly as in the name, a street. The cool thing is there is not much traffic that goes through the street. Lining the street is stores, restaurants, ice cream shops, and museums. On Wisconsin

IMAGE

August 18 2019

We went to a Badger football game today! Watching the whole statium sway to Varsity together was special. Varsity is the alma mater for the Wisconsin Badgers and it is sung after any event or game. I also loved seeing all the college students dressed in the red and white striped overalls. School spirit is phenomanal. The city itself felt alive. Streets were filled and there didn't seem to be any parking avaliable for miles. After the game we found more curds and thank goodness we had our walking shoes on. We then walked what seemed like miles to the Capitol building. The landcaping and structure was cool to see.
